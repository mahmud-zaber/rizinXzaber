To My Beautiful Girl ❤️

This page isn't just an apology. It's everything I should have said, everything I should have understood, and everything I want you to know from my heart.

I'm Sorry ❤️

My love, I know I wasn't there for you the way I should have been. During a time when you were already dealing with pain, discomfort, exhaustion, and emotions that can feel overwhelming, you deserved more understanding, more patience, and more support from me. Looking back, I realize that I didn't give you the comfort you needed. I didn't check on you enough. I didn't make you feel as cared for as you deserved. And for that, I am truly sorry. Not because I was forced to say it. Not because I want to fix things quickly. But because I genuinely understand that I hurt someone I deeply love.

What I Want You To Know 🌸

Even on the days when you feel tired, emotional, irritated, exhausted, or completely drained... You never become difficult to love. You never become a burden. You never become "too much." You deserve kindness. You deserve patience. You deserve understanding. Most importantly, you deserve someone who stays beside you through all of it. I want to be that person.
